ABOUT US

DRESSES BY AISHA is a brand borne out of concern for muslim women.
Over the years, getting modest female clothing had been a big issue.
I would frequently visit the market to shop for myself and my kids but
only return with few and mostly ugly outfits.
I decide to tap into the tailoring skills I had acquired a while back to
solve this problem.
We at DRESSES BY AISHA pride ourselves in creating stylish pieces that
are still very much modest and does not go against the muslim woman dress code.
We have also made our oufits very affordable because
we want to see muslim women look good without breaking the bank.
We also have lots of options to pick from and our oufits are versatile.
